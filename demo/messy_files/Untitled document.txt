Interview Questions — Senior Product Manager Role

SCREENING (30 min, recruiter):
1. Walk me through your background and what attracted you to this role.
2. What's your experience with B2B SaaS products?
3. Tell me about a product you launched from concept to delivery.
4. What's your salary expectation?
5. When would you be available to start?

HIRING MANAGER (45 min):
1. Describe a time you had to make a tough prioritization decision. What framework did you use?
2. How do you gather and synthesize customer feedback into product requirements?
3. Tell me about a feature that failed. What did you learn?
4. How do you work with engineering teams to balance tech debt vs. new features?
5. Walk me through how you'd approach building a roadmap for a new product area.

CROSS-FUNCTIONAL (45 min, design + eng):
1. How do you collaborate with designers during the discovery phase?
2. Describe your ideal relationship with engineering leads.
3. Tell me about a time you had a disagreement with a stakeholder. How did you resolve it?
4. How do you define success metrics for a feature launch?

EXECUTIVE (30 min, VP Product):
1. Where do you see B2B product management heading in the next 3 years?
2. How do you think about competitive positioning?
3. Tell me about the biggest impact you've had on a business metric.
4. What questions do you have about our product strategy?

EVALUATION CRITERIA:
- Strategic thinking (can they see the big picture?)
- Customer empathy (do they talk about user needs?)
- Execution skills (can they ship?)
- Collaboration (how do they work with others?)
- Communication (clear, structured, concise?)
