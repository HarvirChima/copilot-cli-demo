Vendor Evaluation — Project Management Tools

Evaluated by: Operations Team, March 2025
Purpose: Replace current tool (Basecamp) with something that better supports cross-team workflows

OPTION A: Asana Business
- Cost: $24.99/user/month (est. $7,500/month for 300 users)
- Pros: Great timeline views, strong integrations, good mobile app
- Cons: Reporting is limited without premium tier, learning curve for non-technical teams
- Trial feedback: 7.5/10 average from pilot group

OPTION B: Monday.com Enterprise
- Cost: $19/user/month base + $5/user for enterprise features (est. $7,200/month)
- Pros: Very visual, easy to customize, good automations
- Cons: Can feel overwhelming with too many boards, search is weak
- Trial feedback: 8.1/10 average from pilot group

OPTION C: Linear
- Cost: $8/user/month (est. $2,400/month)
- Pros: Fast, clean UI, great for engineering workflows, excellent keyboard shortcuts
- Cons: Less suited for non-engineering teams, limited project views, no built-in time tracking
- Trial feedback: 8.8/10 from eng, 5.2/10 from ops and marketing

OPTION D: Notion + Notion Projects
- Cost: $10/user/month (est. $3,000/month)
- Pros: Flexible, combines docs + projects + wiki, good templates
- Cons: Performance with large databases, not purpose-built for project management
- Trial feedback: 7.9/10 average

RECOMMENDATION: Monday.com Enterprise
- Best balance of usability across all teams
- Strongest automation features (reduces manual status updates)
- Competitive pricing
- Migration plan: 4-week rollout with team-by-team onboarding
